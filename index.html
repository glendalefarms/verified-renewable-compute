How it works

AI, explained without the jargon.

AI is a digital assistant that can read, write, summarise, search and analyse. Compute power is the engine that runs it — and we supply that engine, powered by renewable energy, without ever seeing your data.

Light tasks → a small engine

Writing an email, summarising a page, answering a quick question. Modest compute, low cost.

Heavy work → a bigger engine

Searching thousands of documents or running a model for a whole team. More compute, more power — still renewable.
